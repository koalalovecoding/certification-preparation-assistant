## Recommended Background

### Recommended Profile
This exam is targeted to anyone looking to familiarise themselves with the fundamentals of security, compliance, and identity (SCI) across cloud-based and related Microsoft services. Suitable for business stakeholders, new or existing IT professionals, and students.

### Experience Requirements
- Familiarity with Microsoft Azure and Microsoft 365
- Interest in Microsoft security, compliance, and identity solutions
